01

Efficio.

Our primary product is a patented middleware solution called Efficio. It is the basis of our ability to leverage any device from any system across a simple holistic platform. Quite simply, Efficio lets any NUI device - from Leap to Kinect to Oculus Rift - interact seamlessly.

Efficio’s rapid prototyping dramatically reduces complexity of integrating disparate systems. The limitations of one-to-one, mutually exclusive interactions are alleviated, creating a device agnostic platform that reduces CAPEX and Total Cost of Ownership (TCO.)

Efficio is more than an integration tool – it understands user actions and intent, enhancing usability. By hooking existing functionality to a collection of predefined and custom gestures, it adds functionality while easing the interaction andf control of applications users have learned and adopted. Voice commands can be processed as well.

The middleware offers a significant enhancement for 3D software applications – such as Maya, Sketchup, TinkerCad or 3DMax - that can’t natively support an NUI interface. Instead of having to develop 2D models for rendering, Efficio allows users to actually work in 3D. Not only does this significantly increase productivity but also employee satisfaction. In highly technical fields, that’s a significant metric in talent retention.

Efficio lets every headset, every device, every system interact – encouraging creativity in health care, defense, rescue and recovery, telecommunications or social media. NUI devices become a commercially-available entry way for transforming how companies communicate, deliver services, solve problems and encourage creativity.

About Us.

Abantech is an entrepreneurial firm created to conceptualize, enhance and develop solutions using Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Mixed Reality (VR| AR|MR.)

We enjoy cool code as much as anyone, but our focus is finding better ways for humans to interact with systems by creating 3D environments that aren’t constricted by an intermediary device.

  • Improve access with device agnostic platform
  • Increase adoption by connecting Natural User Interface (NUI) across mutually exclusive systems
  • Extend 3D capacity for health care, defense, NUI hardware and 3D software.
Our patented middleware – Efficio allows NUI devices to connect to any electronic application, system or device. Spatial data from multiple endpoints can fused into a real-time 3D model, using gestures or voice commands.

We integrate COTS (commercial-off-the-shelf-tech) to support the rapid development and adoption of cost effective solutions in 3D. Abantech will help you conceive and create tools that take business operations to a new dimension.
